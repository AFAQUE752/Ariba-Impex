Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Important: At Ariba Impex, we strive for complete customer satisfaction. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and our procedures for returns and refunds.

1. Return Eligibility

1.1 Standard Products

For our standard product range (school bags, jute bags, foam bags), returns are accepted under the following conditions:

  • Products must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition
  • All original tags, labels, and packaging must be intact
  • Products must not show any signs of use, wear, or damage
  • Receipt or proof of purchase must be provided

1.2 Custom Orders

Custom-designed or personalized products are not eligible for return or refund except in cases of:

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Incorrect specifications (not matching approved design)
  • Damage during shipping

1.3 Bulk Orders

For bulk orders, special return conditions may apply. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery to report any issues. A sample inspection may be required before processing returns for large quantities.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom-designed or personalized products (unless defective)
  • Products damaged due to misuse or improper handling
  • Items without original packaging or tags
  • Products that show signs of use or wear
  • Clearance or final sale items (if specified at time of purchase)

3. Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective or damaged product:

  • Contact us immediately upon receiving the product (within 48 hours)
  • Provide clear photographs of the defect or damage
  • Include your order number and purchase details
  • Do not use or further damage the product

We will arrange for replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you. Return shipping for defective products will be covered by Ariba Impex.

4. Return Process

Step 1: Contact Us

Initiate your return by contacting us:

  • Email: info@aribaimpex.in
  • Phone: +91-9903101456

Provide your order number, reason for return, and product details.

Step 2: Return Authorization

Once your return request is approved, you will receive:

  • Return authorization number (RMA)
  • Return shipping instructions
  • Return address details

Step 3: Package and Ship

Package the items securely in their original packaging. Include:

  • Return authorization number (RMA)
  • Copy of receipt or invoice
  • Reason for return

Note: Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Step 4: Inspection and Processing

Once we receive your return:

  • We will inspect the product within 3-5 business days
  • You will receive an email confirmation of receipt
  • If approved, refund will be processed within 7-10 business days

5. Refund Process

5.1 Refund Method

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method:

  • Bank transfer: 5-7 business days
  • Credit/Debit card: 7-14 business days (depending on bank)
  • UPI/Digital wallets: 3-5 business days

5.2 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in the following situations:

  • Items showing minor signs of use
  • Items returned without original packaging (deduction applied)
  • Items returned after the 7-day return window (at our discretion)
  • Bulk orders with partial defects (only defective items refunded)

5.3 Shipping Costs

Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

6. Exchanges

We accept exchanges for:

  • Different size (if available)
  • Different color (if available)
  • Defective items

To request an exchange, follow the return process and specify your exchange preference. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

7. Quality Guarantee

All our products come with a quality guarantee:

  • School Bags: 6 months warranty on stitching and materials
  • Jute Bags: 3 months warranty on handles and seams
  • Foam Bags: 6 months warranty on structural integrity

Warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, misuse, or damage from accidents.

8. Cancellations

8.1 Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled free of charge if:

  • Cancellation request is made within 24 hours of placing the order
  • The order has not yet been dispatched

8.2 Custom Order Cancellation

For custom orders:

  • If cancelled before production begins: Full refund minus 10% processing fee
  • If cancelled after production begins: No refund (non-refundable deposit)

9. Lost or Damaged in Transit

If your order is lost or damaged during shipping:

  • Report the issue within 48 hours of expected delivery
  • We will file a claim with the shipping carrier
  • We will arrange for replacement or full refund

Please inspect your package upon delivery and note any visible damage on the delivery receipt.
