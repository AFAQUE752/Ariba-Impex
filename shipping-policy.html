Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Note: We are committed to delivering your orders safely and on time. This policy outlines our shipping procedures, delivery timelines, and related terms.

1. Shipping Locations

1.1 Domestic Shipping

We currently ship to all locations within India, including:

  • All major cities and metropolitan areas
  • Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities
  • Rural areas (subject to courier serviceability)
  • Union Territories

1.2 International Shipping

International shipping is available for bulk orders on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us for:

  • International shipping rates
  • Customs documentation requirements
  • Estimated delivery times
  • Import duties and taxes information

2. Shipping Methods

We partner with trusted logistics providers to ensure safe delivery:

Standard Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
  • Tracking: Available
  • Best For: Small to medium orders

Express Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 2-4 business days
  • Tracking: Real-time updates
  • Best For: Urgent orders
  • Additional charges apply

Bulk Order Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 7-14 business days (depends on quantity)
  • Method: Freight/Cargo services
  • Best For: Large quantity orders
  • Custom shipping arrangements available

3. Processing Time

3.1 Standard Products

  • In-stock items: 1-2 business days
  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM IST are processed the same day
  • Weekend orders processed on next business day

3.2 Custom Orders

  • Production time: 7-21 days (depending on specifications)
  • Timeline will be confirmed at time of order
  • Rush orders may be accommodated with additional fees

3.3 Bulk Orders

  • Processing time varies based on quantity
  • Estimated timeline provided during quotation
  • Partial shipments available for very large orders

4. Shipping Costs

4.1 Standard Shipping Rates

4.2 Express Shipping

Additional ₹150-300 based on location and weight

4.3 Bulk Orders

Shipping costs calculated based on weight, dimensions, and destination. Free shipping may be available for orders above certain quantities.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive:

  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • SMS notification with courier details
  • Link to track your shipment in real-time
  • Expected delivery date

You can also track your order by:

  • Visiting the courier company's website
  • Contacting our customer service
  • Checking your order status on our website (if applicable)

6. Delivery Process

6.1 Delivery Attempts

Our courier partners will make up to 3 delivery attempts. If delivery fails after 3 attempts, the package will be returned to us. Reshipment will incur additional charges.

6.2 Delivery Requirements

  • A valid phone number must be provided
  • Complete and accurate delivery address is required
  • Recipient or authorized person must be available to receive the package
  • ID verification may be required for high-value orders

6.3 Inspection Upon Delivery

We recommend you:

  • Inspect the package for visible damage before accepting
  • Note any damage on the delivery receipt
  • Open and verify contents within 48 hours
  • Report any issues immediately

7. Delivery Delays

While we strive for timely delivery, delays may occur due to:

  • Natural disasters or severe weather conditions
  • Political unrest or strikes
  • Festival seasons or peak business periods
  • Incorrect or incomplete address information
  • Remote or difficult-to-access locations
  • Customs clearance (for international orders)

In case of delays, we will notify you promptly and provide updated delivery estimates.

8. Undeliverable Packages

Packages may be returned to us if:

  • Recipient refuses delivery
  • Address is incorrect or incomplete
  • Recipient is unavailable after multiple attempts
  • Customs clearance fails (international orders)

In such cases:

  • We will contact you to arrange redelivery
  • Additional shipping charges will apply for reshipment
  • Refunds will be processed minus shipping costs

9. Lost or Damaged Shipments

9.1 Lost Packages

If your package is lost in transit:

  • Report within 7 days of expected delivery
  • We will initiate an investigation with the courier
  • If not located within 15 days, we will process a replacement or refund

9.2 Damaged Shipments

If you receive a damaged package:

  • Do not accept the delivery if outer packaging is severely damaged
  • If accepted, report damage within 48 hours
  • Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • We will arrange for replacement at no cost

10. Special Delivery Instructions

You can provide special delivery instructions such as:

  • Preferred delivery time window
  • Alternative contact person
  • Specific delivery location (office, warehouse, etc.)
  • Gate code or access instructions

Please note that while we share these instructions with our courier partners, we cannot guarantee they will always be followed.

11. Holidays and Non-Business Days

Shipping and delivery times exclude:

  • Sundays and public holidays
  • Company holidays (as announced)
  • Festival periods (Diwali, Holi, etc.)

Orders placed during holidays will be processed on the next business day.
